Restaurants, Cooking, and Cuisine

Cooking has always been a big part of my life. I learned how to cook at a young age, and grew up making Swedish family recipes with my mom and my grandmother.

Some of my favorite dishes to cook are Swedish meatballs, a delicious tahini kale salad, sundried tomato pasta, roasted Brussels sprouts, and pesto salmon.

In addition to cooking, I love to try new restaurants, both near and far.

In Madison, I have tried numerous restaurants and have narrowed it down to my top 5 to mention here. They are as follows:

Restaurant Rating out of 10 Favorite Dish
Eno Vino 9.5/10 Wild Mushroom Risotto
Tavernakaya 9/10 General Tso's Cauliflower
Cento 9.5/10 Salmon alla Toscana
Short Stack 9.25/10 "Trio" Brunch Plate
