Travel

I love to travel and have been fortunate to live in major cities in between my travels. I have included images and descriptions of a few of my favorite trips on this site.

My Home Cities

Chicago, Illinois

I live in Chicago part-time with my mom. I love the city - it feels manageable while still being a hub of culture. There is always something to do.

I love to go running on the Lakefront path, try new restaurants, and go shopping.

New York, New York

I live in New York part-time with my dad. This is my favorite city of all time.

It truly has a feel like nowhere else on earth.

Washington, D.C.

I also spend part of my time in Washington, D.C., with my dad.

As our nation's capital city, it is lively and never has a lack of exciting things going on.

Ireland

I visited Ireland in 2023. My dad's side of the family is all from there, and it was so amazing to connect with my heritage.

Some of our family live on a farm there, so I got to meet real animals which was an experience unlike any other.

Furthermore, Ireland has such a rich culture through music, food, its main cities, and more. I adored this trip.

Lisbon

I visited Lisbon in 2022. Located in the country of Portugal, Lisbon is a major city that is filled with incredible history.

I explored old castles with my family, as well as went on long walks exploring the city. I would highly recommend visiting Lisbon.
